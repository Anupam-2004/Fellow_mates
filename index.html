Key Features

  • Student Information Management: Centralized records, profiles, attendance tracking.
  • Admission Management: Online applications, status tracking, merit lists.
  • Attendance Management: Digital marking, automated reports, parent notifications.
  • Timetable Management: Schedule creation, teacher allocation, easy viewing.
  • Examination and Grading: Online/offline exams, mark entry, report cards.
  • Fee Management: Online payments, due tracking, automated reminders.
  • Communication and Collaboration: Messaging, notifications, parent portal.
  • Learning Management System (LMS) Integration: Study materials, assignments, online classes.
  • Library Management: Cataloging, issue/return tracking, fine management.
  • Hostel Management: Resident data, fee management, attendance (if applicable).
  • Transport Management: Route management, tracking, fee management (if applicable).
  • Human Resources (HR) and Payroll Management: Staff records, attendance, payroll.
  • Financial Accounting: Income/expense management, financial reports.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Customizable reports, data analysis dashboards.
  • Security and Data Backup: Secure storage, regular backups.
  • Multi-language Support: Accessibility in multiple languages.
